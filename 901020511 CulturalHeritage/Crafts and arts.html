Crafts and Arts

As mentioned earlier that Basotho culture is one of the greatest cultures in the world,they have crafts such as woven baskets,pottery and beadwork and they are designed in such a way that is very extraordinary.This designs are mostly made by women and few men,below we will provide pictures and definations based on the mentioned crafts and arts.In this culture every boy or men becomes a sheperd and they have to look after animals all day while women stay at home preparing food,looking after children as well as cleaning, so women sometimes completes their duties fast and as a result they get bored so they had to find a way to remove their boredom that is why they end up designing this interesting crafts and arts.Minority of men design them as something they will earn something from maybe selling them to earn money

Woven Basket

The above craft shows a woven basket it is mostly designed by Basotho women using materials such as grass and reeds to create a beautiful and eye catching basket,and they are sometimes decorated by excellent patterns and designs.

Pottery

This is a pottery it is designed using clays and other techniques,They use natural materials like wood ash and plant dyes to create unique glazes.This pottery is known for its earthy tones and traditional techniques.

Beadwork

Basotho beadwork is a vibrant and colorful craft used to adorn jewellery,clothing and other decorative items.It is often known for featuring interesting patterns and designs using a variety of colors and techniques.This craft is mostly worn by women and girls.
