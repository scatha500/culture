Basotho Music and Dance

Basotho culture has multiple interesting dances and music which include mohobelo,mokhibo,ntlamo,litolobonya and many more.On the side of music they have famo music,mangae and many others features in this incredible culture. In this page we will showcase the rich cultural heritage of the Basotho people.

Below is the list of traditional dances as well as instruments and their descriptions.

1.Traditional Dances

Mohobelo

This is a traditional dance that involves foot movement perfomed by men for entertainment and amazement.

Mokorotlo

A powerful dance for men,it is often perfomed by the chief on important occasions.

Mokhibo

A dance perfomed by women shown by movement of shoulders and kneeling down.

Likakatso

A dance involving energetic movements and rhythemic chanting

Lekhakha

A traditional Basotho dance that features movements

Basotho cultural dance

A traditional dance that showcases the rich cultural heritage uf the Basotho people'

Mabele

A traditional dance that shows rhythemic chanting.

Basotho Folk dance

A traditional dance that involves storyteling through movement and music.

Ntlamo

A traditional Basotho dance mostly perfomed by boys to show culture.

Litolobonya

A traditional dance perfomed by women on big ceremonies.

Liphotha

A dance perfomed by boys to tease and amaze the audience.

2.Traditional Instruments

Lekholulo

A flute traditionally played by herding boys.

Setolo-tolo

A large, extended jaw harp played by men using their mouth.

Thomo

A stringed instrument played by women.

Lesiba

A flute played by herding boys for personal emjoyment and to soothe cattle.

Mamokhorong

a more recent addition, this bowed monochord instrument,often with a tin can resonator,played by herders.

Moropa

A drum used in ceremonies and celebrations for producing large sound effects.

Sekoroana

A traditional Basotho instrument similar to a flute

Mokorotlo

A traditional Basotho hat worn during ceremonies and celebrations

Khanje

A traditional instument similar to a guitar.

Kokoana

A traditional Basotho instrument similar to a fiddle

Liphusoana

An instrument similar to a whistle.

Moriting

A traditional Basotho instrument similar to a xylophone
